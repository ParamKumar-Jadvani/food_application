# Food Application

This food application provides users with a variety of recipes to explore, save to favorites, and add to meal plans. Users can view detailed information about each recipe, including ingredients, instructions, and nutritional information.

## Features

- **Home Page**: Browse through a selection of recipes.
- **Recipe Details**: View detailed information about each recipe, including ingredients, instructions, difficulty level, and ratings.
- **Favorites**: Save your favorite recipes for easy access later.
- **Meal Planning**: Add recipes to your meal plan to keep track of what you want to cook.
- **Nutritional Information**: Check the calorie count and other nutritional details of each recipe.

## Usage

1. **View Recipes**: Browse through the recipes and click on any recipe to view its details.
2. **Add to Favorites**: Click on the "Add to Favorite" button to save a recipe to your favorites.
3. **Add to Meal Plan**: Click on the "Add to Meal" button to add a recipe to your meal plan.
4. **Start Cooking**: Follow the instructions on the recipe detail page to cook your selected dish.
